Butternut Squash Soup

Return to Recipes

Description

This creamy soup is perfect for the fall weather.

Ingredients

  • 1 butternut Squash
  • 1 onion
  • 4 cups vegetable stock
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp thyme
  • 1 tbsp sage
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Milk or heavy cream
  • 1/2 tsp ground pepper

Steps

  1. Put the oil in a large pot with diced onions. Cook on medium high until onions begin to soften.
  2. Dice up the butternut squash and place in the pot. Cook until beginning to soften.
  3. Add sage, thyme, salt and pepper, cook for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add vegetable stock. Bring soup to a boil. Cover and shimmer until butternut squash is completely softened.
  5. Add soup to a blender to create a creamy soup. Add milk or heavy cream, usually 1/4 is all it needs, as desired.
  6. Add salt as desired and enjoy with corn bread!
