Eggs & Tortillas

Return to Recipes

Description

Good breakfast to keep you full from the healthy fat of the eggs and some carbohydrates from the tortillas.

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • Tortillas
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Cook your eggs as desired in a pan.
  2. Warm up the tortillas in a pan to desired temperature or in the microwave for 10-15 seconds.
  3. Put the eggs and tortillas on a plate.
  4. Add ketchup to the top of the eggs.
  5. Enjoy your breakfast.
